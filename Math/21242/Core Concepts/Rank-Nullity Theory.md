## 1 Rank

The rank is the dimension of the image (range) of a [[Linear Transformation]]. 

## 2 Nullity

The nullity is the dimension of the kernel (null space) of a [[Linear Transformation]]. 

## 3 Rank-Nullity Theory

For the matrix representation ${} A: m times n {}$ of a linear transformation $T: FF^n -> FF^m$, the following holds:

$$
1. op("rank")(A) + op("nullity")(A) = n \
2. op("rank")(A^top) + op("nullity")(A^top) = m \
op("rank")(A) = dim(op("Im") A) = dim(op("Im") A^top) \
op("nullity")(A) = dim(op("Ker") A) \
op("nullity")(A^top) = dim(op("Ker") A^top)
$$
