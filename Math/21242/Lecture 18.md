## 1 Inner Product

$angle.l dot, dot angle.r : V times V -> FF$

1. Conjugate-symmetry: $angle.l arrow(v), arrow(w) angle.r = overline(angle.l w\, v angle.r)$
2. Linearity: $angle.l alpha arrow(u) + beta arrow(v), arrow(w) angle.r = alpha angle.l arrow(u), arrow(w) angle.r + beta angle.l arrow(v), arrow(w) angle.r$
3. Non-negtivity: $angle.l arrow(v), arrow(v) angle.r >= 0$
4. Positive-definity: $angle.l arrow(v), arrow(v) angle.r = 0 ==> arrow(v) = arrow(0)$
