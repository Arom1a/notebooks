## 1 Product of vectors
### 1.1 Dot product
$\vec{a}\cdot \vec{b}=||\mathbf{\vec{a}}||\, ||\vec{b}||\, \cos{\theta}$

### 1.2 Cross product
The value of cross product is: $||\vec{a}\times \vec{b}||=||\vec{a}||\, ||\vec{b}||\, |\sin{\theta}|$
