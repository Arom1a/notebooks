## 1 Bitwise Operator
`&` means 'AND'. 
`|` means 'OR'. 
`~` means 'NOT'. 
`^` means 'XOR'. 
`<<` means 'left shift'. 
`>>` means 'right shift'. 

## 2 Logic Operator
`&&` means 'logical and'. 
`||` means 'logical or'. 
`!` means 'logical not'. 

## 3 Boolean Algebra
### 3.1 De Morgan's Laws
`!(a&&b) == !a || !b`
`!(a||b) == !a && !b`
### 3.2 Distributive Law of Boolean Algebra
`a || (b&&c) == (a||b) && (a||c)`
`a && (b||c) == (a&&b) || (a&&c)`
